Home Entertainment Stand

Entertainment stand for TV and home consoles with built-in cable management.

What?

  • Wooden entertainment stand designed for a Sony XBR-55A8G TV and X-Box with built-in cable management

How?

  • Produced 3D CAD models in Creo then used static FEA analysis to ensure the stand met specifications

Results

  • Stand is easily capable of supporting over 100lb of unevenly distributed weight making it sufficient for the average consumer's entertainment needs
